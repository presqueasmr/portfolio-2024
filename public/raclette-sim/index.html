Raclette Simulator

OPEN THE PROJECT

PROBLÉMATIQUE

This project was requested by my boss as a way to learn FlutterFlow. The goal create a fun, creative raclette shopping-list app. I created this app from benchmark, to design, to development, to deploying in stores.

LET'S THINK FOR 5 MINUTES...

MAIN PAINS

  • The app's goal is to be fun and engaging
  • The algorithm should be accurate for the app to be usable

SOLUTIONS

  • Create a sweet and simple user flow with cool assets and play on words
  • I could have done better but the algoritms do work.
LET'S US COOK...

SOLUTIONS

I started by getting inspiration for some assets, to get the feel, the general idea of the app. I then got to do a little benchmark. The art direction would be classical warm fast food colors (red-orange-yellow) with cartoonish drawings and thick oulines giving a retro feel to the project.

The second step was to design the first screens <that I updated recently after some user tests>.

I then got around to developing the app with Flutterflow and create the algorithm.

Finally the hardest part was to deploy the app on the Play Store and the App Store following all the restrictions and delays.
